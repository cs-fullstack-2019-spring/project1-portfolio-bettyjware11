About Me

I was born and raised in Memphis, TN. I grew up on the South side of town and graduated from Whitehaven High School. During those days, I was really into Spelling, Math and Art. I loved art so much that I took advanced placement art in the 12th grade and competed in competitions. I did a variety of things from oil painting to charcoal drawings. I even got into air brushing design for tee shirts and other items that people wanted to add that personal touch to.

My artistic nature also led to me experimenting with different hairstyles and colors. I have tried several ideas on myself, family members and friends. One thing that I have found, however, is that some people are afraid to try a new style or hair color without actually knowing how it will look on them beforehand. To that extent, I plan to build an app that will allow a person to see a style before actually committing to it.

I received college credit for the AP art class and went to the University of Memphis on a scholarship. Once there, however, my interests turned to a more practical course and I pursued Accounting as a major. I did this for two reasons. I loved Math and I was convinced by those around me that I could not make money in the field of Art. I was told that I needed to find another subject that I could actually get a job with. This is what I did. I graduated with a Bachelors and went to work. I stayed in the field of Accounting for over a decade before I finally became burnt out with the political nature of the job I had. I knew it was time to get back to my artistic roots; perhaps even merging my Accounting skills with my artistic nature to build a platform...
